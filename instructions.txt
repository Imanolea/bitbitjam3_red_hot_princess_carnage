Game Boy game made by Light Games for BitBitJam3
Imanol (Imanolea), Belinda (Leia83), Sergio (LocoMJ)

Controls
***********
Left/Right - Look left/right
Down - Crouch
A - Jump
B - Stab

Story
***********
The Prophecy says that every thousand years Koga Ninja try to steal Hino's family jewel. 
The time has come. Akaru-hime, also known as The Red Princess, is willing to protect her 
family's treasure, even with her own blood...
